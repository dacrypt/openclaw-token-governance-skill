# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on technical issues, not people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or abusive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or
PRs that violate this Code of Conduct.
